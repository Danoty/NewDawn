About NewDawn School

Excellence, character, and innovation at the shores of Lake Victoria.

Who We Are

A Beacon of Academic & Character Excellence

NewDawn School is a dynamic learning community offering holistic education from Kindergarten to Year 12. Located in the heart of Bondo, Siaya County, we strive to nurture each learner academically, socially, emotionally, and spiritually.

We combine inquiry-based learning, modern facilities, and a caring environment to help our pupils discover their strengths, explore their passions, and unlock their God-given potential.
