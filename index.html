About NewDawn School

Excellence, character, and innovation at the shores of Lake Victoria.

Who We Are

A Beacon of Academic & Character Excellence

NewDawn School is a dynamic learning community offering holistic education from Kindergarten to Year 12. Located in the heart of Bondo, Siaya County, we strive to nurture each learner academically, socially, emotionally, and spiritually.

We combine inquiry-based learning, modern facilities, and a caring environment to help our pupils discover their strengths, explore their passions, and unlock their God-given potential.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions from parents and guardians.

What curriculum do you follow?

We implement the Kenyan CBC pathway in the early and middle years, with IGCSE and A-Level options in high school.

Do you provide school transport?

Yes, transport is available on designated routes. Please contact the office for current coverage and fees.

How can I start the admission process?

Call or WhatsApp us, or submit the inquiry form below. Our office will guide you step-by-step.

Do you offer scholarships or financial support?
